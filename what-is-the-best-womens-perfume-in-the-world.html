    Chanel No. 5

    The Chanel No. 5 perfume is without a doubt the most famous fragrance in the world. It was created by Gabrielle Chanel in 1921 as a personal signature scent for herself and her boutique. It quickly became a favorite among women everywhere and remains one of the most popular fragrances to this day. The timeless scent is made up of a variety of fresh, luxurious ingredients, including lavender, citrus, bergamot, oakmoss, and sandalwood.
